Frequently Asked Questions for Training Companies

In what framework is an assignment possible?

The assignment takes place within several clearly defined practical blocks during the retraining to become a specialist for application development. The timeframes are long-term planable and can be coordinated early on.

What tasks can I take over?

Participation in web and software projects, implementation of clearly defined development tasks, support in frontend and backend topics as well as testing and technical documentation.

How high is the supervision effort?

New technologies and internal processes I work through systematically and independently. Questions are asked specifically and solution-oriented, to keep the supervision effort low.

How is the compensation regulated?

The assignment takes place within several clearly defined practical blocks during the retraining to become a specialist for application development. The compensation is paid by the responsible educational institution. No personnel costs arise for the practice company.

Is there an obligation to offer permanent employment?

No. There is no obligation to offer permanent employment. The focus lies on a professionally meaningful collaboration during the practical phases.
